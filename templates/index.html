About DomAi

DomAi is a web-based AI platform that predicts both the domain (Art, Real, Clipart, Product) and class (e.g., Bottle, Chair, Pen) of an image using advanced Unsupervised Domain Adaptation (UDA) techniques.

UDA lets AI models adapt to new types of images without extra labels. DomAi leverages datasets like Office-Home to generalize across domains.
